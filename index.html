Selected Projects

Solenoid Valve — Design & Modeling

Tools: CAD, Basic Electronics

Objective:
Develop a compact solenoid-actuated valve to study electromagnetic actuation and fluid control.

Methodology:

  • Modeled the valve body and plunger assembly using CAD software
  • Designed with alignment and tolerance considerations
  • Iteratively analyzed plunger mass and coil specifications

Key Insights:

  • Trade-offs between magnetic force and return spring stiffness
  • Critical role of friction and dimensional tolerances
  • Discrepancy between theoretical models and real-world performance

Next Iteration:
Improvements would include enhanced sealing and guided plunger movement to reduce mechanical losses.
