Projects

Solenoid Valve — Design & Modeling

Tools: CAD, basic electronics

Problem:
Design a compact solenoid-actuated valve to understand electromagnetic actuation and fluid control.

Approach:

  • Modeled valve body and plunger assembly in CAD
  • Designed around alignment and tolerance constraints
  • Iterated plunger mass and coil assumptions

Key Learnings:

  • Magnetic force vs. return spring trade-offs
  • Importance of friction and tolerances
  • Gap between ideal models and real behavior

If I rebuilt it:
I would improve sealing and plunger guidance to reduce losses.
